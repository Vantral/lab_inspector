Nowadays the quick growth of the number of health problems is supposed to be connected with the increasing use of devices. More and more people claim or headaches or heart diseases and hospitals are always full of clients. This essay proves that technologies cause many health problems today. 
First of all, devices allow to stay home as much as possible. From your seat you can do shopping, learn, order food and so on. All this possibilities cause the lack of moving. That is why many modern people, including children, suffer from overweight. Your smartphone or tablet take your motivation to move, walk, do sport. However, there is a solution of this problem and all depend on you. It is necessary to make yourself do easy things independently. You can use any possibility to move: go shopping, walking and so on. 
Secondly, the screens of our devices affect our vision negatively. Our eyes do not relax, they concentrate small letters, pictures and as a result, we watch worse that we must. Specialists advise to take breaks: during your work or another using of technologies it is useful to watch through the window or some things that surround you. 
In addition, the use of devices often cause headaches because of the huge spread of formation. Our brain cannot recycle al the information we receive. So it could be useful to filter what we read, watch or listen to or to take only necessary information that you need at the current moment. 
In conclusion, modern technologies are not something bad. We cannot decline their utility however we should have some limits of their using. Our organs and health mustn't suffer from devices.
