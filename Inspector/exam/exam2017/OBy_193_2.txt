The majority of people think that active use of new gadgets leads to expanding problems with health. This essay will discuss the main problem causing by technology, concerning eye diseases, then it will suggest a possible solution of increasing problem. 
The most noticeable problem, which people can face abusing the use of modern technology, is the decreasing quality of vision. People of different age have to spent a lot of time in front of the computers or iPhones. This does not allow their eyes to move often enough to se well, because the eyes use to see information only nearby, but not in the distance. The research of the Ministry of Health in 2014 showed, that every second pupil had complains on the quality of vision, as they had to work more than 6 hours a day on computer or IPad. Moreover, they watch television after. In addition, others problems appear, that concern obesity. 
A possible solution to this problem is not very special. Firstly, there should be a strict control of parents and teachers. They should limit the time, children spend using their gadgets. Adults may do special exercises every 30 minutes at work. All of people should do sport regulary to prevent the obesity and problems with heart. There is no special measures to improve the increasing problem, people should understand its importance and stop a possible bad influence of technology. 
In conclusion, there is a significant problem, concerning human health and use of technology, but there are different ways to prevent it. People can solve this problem by themselves, if they will take care and pay more attention to the health.
