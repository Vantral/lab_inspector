There is an opinion that new technologies are the reason for raising amount of diseases. The essay will discuss some major health problems and it will develop possible solutions. 
First of all, it is evident that gadgets can cause some health problems for those who use them. The major problems that doctors report about are problems with eyes and with obesity. Eye diseases occure when people are using their electronic devices, especially if gadgets do not have eye-friendly screens or they are used close to eyes. Number o problems with overweigh rises because people became less active due to new technologies. For example, American scientists found out that every third American had problems with eyes in 2010, comparing to 15% in 1990. They said the main reason is the popularity of modern gadgets. Thus, modern technologies really can affect user's health. 
However, these problems can be solved and many states now implement some of the possible solutions. Manufacturers contribute to this by creating new gadgets or software which will stimulate owners to some physical activities, as an example, popular game Pokemon Go allows players to level up only by doing some activities like walking or running. Possible solution for the problems with eyes is to develop new screens which will not be harmful. Governments can help with this by financing researches. That means that these problems can be solved or at least damage can be reduced. 
In conclusion, there are some significant health problems caused by gadgets, but solutions for them can be developed.
