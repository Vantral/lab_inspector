The bar chart illustrates the data on the print and electronic books market in the USA, Germany, China and the UK in 2014 with a prediction for 2018. 
Overall, in 2014 in all four countries the most popular type of books was print. In contrast, in 2018 it is predicted print books will be popular more popular in Germany and China, whereas in the USA and the UK will probably increase in eBook market. Generally speaking, in 2014 Print market hit a peak in the USA (10,5 billion US Dollars). The lowest point of market was in the UK (2,6). However, the eBook market hit a peak in the USA (5,5) and the lowest point was fixed in China (0,5). Moreover, the figure of Germany maintained approximately the same level (1). 
By contrast, in 2018 the peaks of print and eBooks markets are estimated both in the USA (7,5 and 8,5 respectively). EBook market is likely to hit a low in China (1), while print market will probably hit a low in the UK (2). To sum up, a clear rising trend is observed for eBook market.
