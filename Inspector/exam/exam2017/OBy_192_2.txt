This essay agrees that developments have an effect on people's life. Some people say that new inventions could lead to illnesses. Others disagree and say that developments can prevent human beings from getting hurt. 
There is an opinion that modern technologies can make people's life more safe than it was before. First of all there are some inventions, like mobile phone, that can help the user to stay healthy. For instance, a person can download programs in his device which would remind to do physical activities like running. There are also such things that can measure how many calories the user of the phone should ear per day. Furthermore, technologies provide people with sport equipment which can make exercise more effective. For example, there are new types of boots that help to run longer and faster in any weather condition. They are not weight much and long-lasting. 
Nevertheless, some people believes that such technology as a mobile phone can damage eyes and lead to blindness. Despite this fact, modern telephones are totally harmless for people's health because they have lots of programs that prevent such problem. For example, it is possible to change the brightness of the phone, so eyes will not get hurt. 
In conclusion it can be said that modern technologies are totally harmless and can't lead to any illness. Lots of upgrades made such things as mobile phones and "clever" walkers totally harmless to people. Moreover, they help people to stay healthy, strong and not falling ill.
