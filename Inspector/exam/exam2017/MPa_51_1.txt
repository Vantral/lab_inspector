The graphs illustrate the differences between the average maximum and minimum temperatures in different months throughout the year in Yakutsk, Russia and Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. The most noticeable fact is that the amplitude of average temperatures is much higher in Yakutsk compared to Rio de Janeiro. The coldest months in Russia are January and December with an average minimum and maximum temperatures of roughly -40°C and -35°C respectively. While in Brazil the month with the lowest temperature is July when the average minimum temperature is just over +17°C and the average maximum one is about +25°C, which is approximately equal to the average maximum temperature in July in Yakutsk, which in the maximum level of temperature which is reached in this city. While in Rio de Janeiro the temperature reaches its maximum point in January namely with the average maximum and minimum temperatures of 30°C and about 23°C respectively.
