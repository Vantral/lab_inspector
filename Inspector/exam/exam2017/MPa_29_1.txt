The diagram below illustrates how printed books and eBooks market was developed in USA, Germany, China and UK between 2014 and 2018. Overall, electronic books sales used in every country: the most noticeble growth can be spotted in the USA market, where the amout almost doubled from 5.5 Billion US Dollars to 8,5. The smoothiest increase can be seen in China: the sales changed only a half of a billion to 1 billion in 2018. Regarding printed books, there is an opposite trend: almost in every country (except Germany) the sales lowered. More detailed look at the graph reveals that the most stable market is located in the UK, while the unstable are in the USA. To sum up, electronic books market has a huge potential, while a market of printed books loses its positions.
