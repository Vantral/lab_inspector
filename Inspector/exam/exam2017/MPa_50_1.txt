The line graphs provide information about temperatures in Yakutsk and the Rio de Janeiro during the year. The most noticeable fact is that minimum and maximum temperatures in Rio de Janeiro are higher than they are in Yakutsk during the all presented period. Also it can be seen from the graph that average temperature in Yakutsk rises during the first half of the year and reaches its pick in July and then begins to fall rapidly. However, in Rio de Janeiro average temperature flactuate slightly during the year. In Yakutsk the minimum average temperature is fixed in January and maximum is in July. On the contrary, in Rio de Janeiro maximum temperature is fixed in January and minimum is in July. The other markable trend is that average temperature in Yakutsk has a big difference between maximum and minimum level. It is approximately 65°C. However in Rio de Janeiro this difference is small. It is only about 15°C.
