Moreover, the first graph shows that there is a sharp rise of the temperature from January to July in the Russian city, while in the Brazilian one the temperature falls down only for 5°C during the same period. In addition, these two charts perfome that the trend minimum temperature repeats the shape of the line of maximum one. Overall, these two graphs shows big differences in average maximum and minimum temperature between two cities: Yakutsk and Rio de Janeiro.
