The line graphs given for analysis illustrate some statistical measurements of average temperatures in Yakutsk and Rio de Janeiro. Overall, according to the pictures, climate conditions of the subjects completely differ and have close figures only in summer months. It's evident that the gaps between maximum and minimum temperatures in the towns are similar for the whole year period, but may flactuate from 5 to 10 degrees. Other observations show that the previously mentioned one is practically the only common thing in Yakutsk' and Rio de Janeiro's climates. The most significant contrast here is an interval between average temperatures of the absolute maximum and absolute minimum of the towns that is around 60 degrees in Yakutsk and 10 degrees in Brazilian city.
