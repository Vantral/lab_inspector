The chart below provides us the information about changing in the unemployment rate in different regions in 2014 and 2015. 
Overall, we can see that the biggest percentage of unemployements is in North Africa, wile the least one is in South Asia. Moreover, the figures for these regions did not change. They are 12,5% for North Africa and 3,9% for South Asia. The worldwide's unemployment rate is between these figures and also did not change (5,9%). 
However, in other part of world the situation is different. For example, in Middle East or in Latin America positves trends can be observed. Unemployment rate in these regions became lower (from 11% to 9,6% in Middle East, from 6,8% to 5,8% in Latin America). But the situation in European Union is the opposite. There the amount of unemployment grew up in 2015. It was 7,5% in 2014 and 7,9% in 2015.
