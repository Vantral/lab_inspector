The column chart provides information about unemployment rate in selected world regions during the period from 2014 to 2015. 
Overall, it can be seen from the chart that in terms of percentage of unemployed in the world remained steady. However, there was a slight increase in European Union which had 7,5% of unemployed population in 2014 and 7,9% in 2015. Furthermore, downward trend in unemployment rate was in Middle East and Latin America. The first fell down from 11% by approximately one and a half. The latter had dropped marginally from 6,8% to 5,8. 
In conclusion, the remarkably high percentage of unemployed people in North Africa hadn't changed at all. The same tendency had South Asia which is the representative of the lowest unemployment rate.
