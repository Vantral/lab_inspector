The picture below shows data on level of unemployment in different regions in 2014 and 2015. The average percent of unemployment in the world is 5,9. It remains stable during the whole period of analysis. There are also no changes in North Africa and in South Asia. 
Percent of unemployment decreased in the Middle East from 11% to 9,6% and in Latin America from 6,8% to 5,8%. The highest rate is in Africa - 12,5. It is not surprisible, because last 3 years this region is rather unstable. The lowest rate is in South Asia, the percent is 3,9. Experts explain it as an "asian economic miracle", that is why there is small percent of people, who cannot find a job. A negative trend can be noticed in EU, the level of unemployment rised on 0,4%, from 7,5% to 7,9%. the reason for such movement is migration crises in Europe. 
To sum up, there are a positive trend of falling the rate of unemployment in many regions, it can be considered as a development of world economic system. However there is a problem in European Union, the level of unemployment increased and nowadays experts predict that situation is going to become worse.
