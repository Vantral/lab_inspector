This chart describes changes in the unemployment rate in selected world regions in 2014 and 2015. First of all, it is hard not to mention that the unemployment rate in 2014 and 2015 changed only in 3 selected world regions. In the North Africa, S. Asia and Worldwide unemployment rate is still the same. On 2014 and 2015 we have 12,5% of unemployment for N. Africa, 3,9% for S. Asia and 5,9 Worldwide. Only the EU had a slight increase in the unemployment rate from 7,5 to 7,9%. Other 2 regions Middle East and Latin America had a little decrease in unemployment rate. Latin America from 6,8 to 5,8% and Middle East from 11 to 9,6%. 
To sum up, it is clear that there was no huge difference between unemployment rate in 2014 and in 2015 in selected world regions. This rate only raised in the EU, still the same in N. Africa, S. Asia, Worldwide and had a slight decrease in Middle East and Latin America.
