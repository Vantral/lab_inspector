The graph chart and table provides information about about the number of girls and boys who experienced obesity and another group of males and females who did exercises in Canada from 1985s to 2005. 
Overall, boys are more likely to suffer obesity than girls as well as are prone to exercise more showing the highest ratings in 2005 both in overweight problems and in physical activity. 
In can be clearly seen that both the number of boys and girls who had overweight issues had been increasing from 1985 to 2005 per year, presenting around 15 % in 1985 and 25% in 2005. Concerning the physical exercises, both the boys and girls were doing physical activities in 1900s well equally. Hovewer, in 2005 this number decreased among girls and increased among boys to 87%.
