This chart shows the percentage of boys and girls in Canada from 1985 to 2005 who were overweight and the table gives information on percentage of girls and boys from 1990 to 2005 who regularly did some physical activity. 
From 1985 to 1990 there were more overwight girls than boys. In 1990 57 percent of boys and 51 percent of girls were regularly physically active. In 1995 16 percent of both girls and boys were overweight. Since 1995 to 2005 the percentage of overwight girls was rising steadily while the percentage of overweight boys was increasing more significant. 
From 1985 to 2005 the percentage of overweight kids increased up to 22 and 25 percent for girls and boys relatively though there was a significant rise in percentage of boys and girls who did regular physical activity and by 2005 77 percent of girls and 87 percent of boys were doing regular physical activity.
