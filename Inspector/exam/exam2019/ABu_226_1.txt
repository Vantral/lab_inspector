Overall, science courses are the most popular among youngsters, whereas sport and health activities are the least popular. Meanwhile, people at the age of 40 years old and above are more interested in art courses, in comparison to 26-40 year olds. 
Considering the pie charts, the majority of teenagers of 18-25 year olds are fond of science courses, which accounts for 55%. Meanwhile, less young people are interested in sport and health activities, that represents approximately 15% of them attending this course. 
What is more, in comparison to youngsters, people at the age of 26-40 years old are keen on sports and health courses, making up a significant 62% of participants. However, only 25% of them attend arts courses, while 40 years old and above are the huge majority of participants of arts courses, representing around 45%.
