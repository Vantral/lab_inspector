The tables show the difference between male and female workers of the three spheres of jobs in Indonesia and Australia in 2010. 
Overall, the highest point of all tables is 88 percent of female workers in the services in Australia and the smallest point of all tables is 1 percent of female workers in the agriculture in Australia. 
In 2010 the agriculture is the unpopular sphere of working between all genders in Australia. Also the unpopular type of sphere in Indonesia is the industry - about 15 percent of men and 8 percent of women. The services is the popular sphere in the both countries, but in contrast, in Indonesia it is popular between men, but in Australia it is popular between women.
