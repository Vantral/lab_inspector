The bar chart illustrates the shifts in the unemployment rate in 2014 and in 2015 in some world regions. The horisontal axis shows this locations: N. Africa, Middle East, EU, Latin America, S. Asia and worldwide range. The vertical axis represents the persentage of unemployment. 
As it can be seen from the chart, the highest unemployment both in 2014 and in 2015 is in North Africa. It stayed stable there and it is 12,5 during both time periods. The lowest degree of unemployment is in South Asia and it is 3,9 for both years. The second highest unemployment is in Middle East and it reached 11% in 2014 and dropped to 9,6% in 2015. The diclane is also can be seen in Latin America where unemployment rate changes from 6,8% in 2014 to 5,8% in 2015. The situation in EU differs from the others as unemployment rises there from 7,5% in 2014 to 7,9 in 2015. The average unemployment rate in the world stais unchanged in both years and it is 5,9%.
