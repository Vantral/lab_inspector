The bar chart below gives information about the unemployment rate and how it changed in different world regions in 2014 and 2015. 
Overall, it is clear from the graph that almost in all regions the rate of unemployment decreased or remained stable in 2015. 
Looking into detales, the largest unemployment rate can be seen in Africa with 12,5 percent in 2014 and the same number in 2015. On the opposite, Asia shows the lowest unemployment rate, but the same as African region has the stable unemployment rate in both 2014 and 2015. Moreover, EU region is unique in the point that its percentage of unemployment rate has grown from 7,5% in 2014 to 7,9% in 2015. Finally, Midde East and Latin America both show a drastic decline in unemployment rate and have 9,6% and 5,8% respectively in 2015.
