The following graphs illustrate the statistycal dynamics of obesity in young people from Canada. The timeline for the survey was 20 years period. The common trend is that obesity rate has increased over the years in both genders. In the girls group it has raised overall for 10%, while in boys this number has reached 16%. The staticstics stays flat in the year 1995. The most drastic uprise in obesity rate was made from 90s to 00s which is a phenomenon that might be connected with capitalistic development of the food industry in Canada over that particular decade. The physical excercise chart provides information that boys are generally more involved into actvities in contrast to girls. However, for both groups the percentage of active young people has grown over the years: 26% for girls and 30% for boys respectively. Despite this fact, the obesity rate according to the bar chart hasn't dropped.
