The chart illustrates the amount of girls and boys who had problems with weight in Canada 1990 and 2005 and the number of children who did sports in 1990 and 2005. 
Overall, the percentage of kids who did regular exercise has increased during all period, the number of overweight girls and boys has showed an upward trend. 
In 1990, girls and boys showed almost the same percentage in terms of sport and were 51 and 57 per cent, respectively. 15 years later, in 2005, the amount of boys who did regular physical activity was just under 90 per cent, while the number of girls was 10 per cent less and the percentage was approximately 80 per cent. 
In 1985, the amount of girls and boys who had large weight in Canada was 12 and 11 per cent, respectively. In 1995 their percentage was the same, 16 per cent. But in 2005, the number of boys became 25 percent and girls' was just over 20, while more and more children became interested in doing sports (girls - 77 per cent, boys - 87 per cent).
