In counries such as Germany and New Zeland difference was only 20-25 percent at that time.5 years later the difference started to become less and 10 years later in 1990 income difference in germany was only 10 percent. Still in Japan the difference was too high - 35 percent between men and women income. Situation improved and in 1995 the difference become 20 to 30 percent in countries with better economical situation and 12 percent in countries with little economical problems.
