The graph depicts the data concerning the proportion of male and female employees in three differnet working fields in Indonesia and Australia. 
Overall, the sector of services is equally popular in both countries. 
29% of all men and 43% of all women in Indonesia work in the sphere of Agriculture. The same sector is noticebly less occupied in Australia. Men there account for 3% and women for 1%. The percentage of men involved in the sphere of Industry in Indonesia reaches to the point of 15 and the percentage of women working there is approximately twice less. There are nearly twice more male workers and approximately the same proportion of women in the same sphere in Australia. Male and female workers who are employed in the sphere os services in both countries account to more than half of a popultion.
