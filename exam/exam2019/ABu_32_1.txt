The chart illustrates the percentage of boys and girls who suffered from overweighting in Canada from 1985 to 2005, as well as the amount of boys and girls who did sports regularly in the late 19s and early 20s. 
According to the bar chart the percentage of the overweight kids significantly increased since 1985 from nearly 10% to almost 25%. However, there is also an upward trend in the amount of boys and girls who did regular physical activities.

In the 2000s, however, the amount of boys suffering from overeating increased more than the percentage of girls and was at the level of 25% in 2005.
