The bar chart shows a percentage of unemployment in different world regions during two years, 2014 and 2015. 
As chart illustrates, North Africa has the highest position in this rate with 12,5% of unemployed people during both years. Middle East is on the second place, but we can see decreasing of unemployment in 2015, it has dropped from 11% to 9,6%. Also we can see a decrease in Latin America, where unemployment became smaller for 1%. The lowest unemployment rate has South Asia, but, as in the North Africa, it stays at the same level both 2014 and 2015 years. The single region in this chart where number of unemployed people increased is EU. Unemployment rate there rose from 7,5% to 7,9%. But, however, the worldwide level of unemployment was stable for this two years. Despite of high percentage of unemployment in some regions, there is a low level of people without a job over the world.
