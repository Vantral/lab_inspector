This histogram shows the information on the print and eBook market in 2014 and with a forecast for 2018 year. In addition, chart shows division in four countries such as USA, Germany, China and the UK. On the one hand, it is clear that development of the book market in the USA represented by far the greatest number of the print market, approximately 2 times, as much as the print market in Germany, which is the next largest number. But, we can see that in 2018 year the eBook market will be decreased. On the other hand, we can also notice that the eBook market of such countries as Germany and China was very low in 2014. Although, in 2018 this market will increase. To sum up, it is clear that the majority of the eBook and Print market will be in the USA. So, this development of the book market was most popular at that time.
